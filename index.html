Welcome to Beyond Earth

Beyond Earth is a web-based project that introduces visitors to the beauty and scale of our solar system and space missions. Designed using only HTML and CSS, this site aims to present key celestial bodies, space organizations, and notable missions in an engaging, beginner-friendly format.

Whether you're passionate about astronomy or just exploring the wonders of the universe, this project captures a glimpse of humanity’s efforts to reach for the stars — from the Moon to Mars and beyond.

Space Missions

Lunar Missions

From Apollo to Chandrayaan, lunar missions have pushed the boundaries of technology and exploration. These missions study the Moon's surface, resources, and potential as a base for future deep-space missions.

Mars Exploration

Mars missions like Perseverance and Curiosity help uncover the planet's history and potential for life. They also test vital technologies for future human colonization.

Deep Space Probes

Voyager and New Horizons have taken us beyond our solar system, sending data from unimaginable distances. These missions expand our knowledge of distant planets and interstellar space.

Discoveries

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life. It has diverse environments, a protective atmosphere, and liquid water — all of which make it habitable.

Mars

Known as the Red Planet, Mars has intrigued scientists with signs of past water and possibly life. Rovers continue to study its surface and atmosphere for future human missions.

Jupiter

The gas giant Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system. Its Great Red Spot is a massive storm, and it hosts dozens of moons, including volcanic Io and oceanic Europa.
